1. Project Title / Headline

Workplace Mental Health & Stress Analytics Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze employee mental health trends, workplace stress factors, and organizational support systems across demographics and regions.

2. Short Description / Purpose

This Power BI dashboard analyzes workplace mental health data to uncover patterns related to stress levels, awareness, and organizational support. It helps stakeholders understand how mental health varies by demographics, company type, and geography to support data-driven HR and wellness decisions.

Mental health awareness and support availability

Comfort levels discussing mental health at work

Medical coverage related to mental health

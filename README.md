## Data Source
Public mental health & workplace survey dataset containing anonymized data on:
- Employee demographics  
- Workplace environment and company type  
- Mental health awareness and support  
- Comfort discussing mental health  
- Medical coverage availability  

## Business Problem
Organizations lack clear visibility into:
- How workplace stress varies across employee segments  
- Whether employees feel comfortable discussing mental health  
- Gaps in awareness and support systems  

Raw survey data makes these insights difficult to extract without structured analysis.

## Dashboard Goals
- Analyze mental health and stress trends across demographics  
- Identify gaps in awareness, support, and coverage  
- Enable evidence-based HR and wellness initiatives  

## Key Features
- **KPI Cards:** Awareness, comfort level, and medical coverage metrics  
- **Demographic Analysis:** Stress trends by age and gender  
- **Geographic Insights:** Country-wise mental health patterns  
- **Company Analysis:** Support comparison across organization types  
- **Awareness vs Comfort:** Relationship between programs and employee openness  

## Business Impact
- **HR Strategy:** Identify high-risk employee segments  
- **Policy Planning:** Support data-driven mental health initiatives  
- **Organizational Culture:** Measure psychological safety and openness
